## About this portfolio

This portfolio demonstrates practical GRC (Governance, Risk & Compliance) and Risk Management skills through two independently developed projects. Each project is built around a realistic fictional company scenario and follows industry-standard frameworks used in real-world compliance and risk management engagements.

## Project 1: ISO 27001:2022 Information Security Policy Framework

**Company:** MediSafe Ltd — a 50-person Dublin-based healthcare technology startup providing a cloud-hosted digital health platform for patients and healthcare providers.

**Framework:** ISO/IEC 27001:2022

**What I built:**
- A complete Information Security Policy Framework covering all four ISO 27001:2022 Annex A control themes: Organisational (A.5), People (A.6), Physical (A.7), and Technological (A.8)
- Policy sections for 58 controls, each with Purpose, Scope, Policy Statement, Responsibilities, and Review Date
- A control mapping and gap analysis table assessing MediSafe's compliance status across all 58 controls
- An executive summary with compliance scorecard, priority findings, and recommended next steps
- Company profile including ISMS scope, data classification model, and asset inventory

**Key findings:**
- 41 controls Compliant (71%)
- 9 controls Partially Compliant (15%)
- 8 controls Non-Compliant (14%)
- 2 Critical findings: Vulnerability Management (A.8.8) and Data Masking (A.8.11)

**Files:**
- `MediSafe_ISO27001_Policy_Framework.docx` — Full policy framework document
- `MediSafe_ISO27001_Control_Mapping.xlsx` — Control mapping and gap analysis table with summary dashboard

## Project 2: Risk Register, Heat Map & Remediation Plan

**Company:** PayFlow Ltd — a 30-person Dublin-based fintech startup providing a cloud-based payment processing platform for SME clients across Ireland and the EU.

**Framework:** ISO 27001:2022 (Clause 6.1.2 — Risk Assessment)

**What I built:**
- A comprehensive risk register identifying 26 risks across 7 categories: Cybersecurity, Operational, Compliance & Regulatory, Third-Party / Vendor, Physical & Environmental, Financial, and People / Human
- Each risk scored on a 5x5 Likelihood x Impact matrix with risk levels (Critical / High / Medium / Low)
- A colour-coded heat map visualising risk distribution across the Likelihood x Impact matrix
- A risk remediation plan documenting prioritised remediation actions for all 26 risks, grouped by severity
- An interactive web-based risk dashboard with live filtering, heat map, category breakdown, and click-to-expand remediation details for each risk

**Risk breakdown:**
- 26 total risks assessed across 7 categories
- 8 Critical risks including: Ransomware, SQL injection, GDPR enforcement, PCI-DSS non-compliance, and payment fraud
- 16 High risks including: Phishing, credential theft, vendor data breach, insider threat, and key person dependency
- 2 Medium risks: Physical access and environmental threats

**Files:**
- `PayFlow_Risk_Register.xlsx` — Full risk register with heat map and summary dashboard
- `PayFlow_Risk_Remediation_Plan.docx` — Prioritised remediation plan for all 26 risks
- `payflow-risk-dashboard.html` — [View the live interactive dashboard](https://bhuvanaks23.github.io/GRC-Portfolio/payflow-risk-dashboard.html)
